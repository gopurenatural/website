About Us

GoPure Ice-Cream is the premium natural ice-cream made out of Fresh Fruits. The ingredients in use are of high quality milk, fruits/dry fruits and sugar only. The Sanctity of the word "Natural" is sought to be preserved to its core, using ingredients that are known to be as pure and as natural as possible.
GoPure is a unique brand focused on natural ice creams and sorbets. The brand is constantly under innovations with new flavours. Our vast and intelligently planned menu helps serve a vast range of delicious products for all sorts of people under a single roof. The brand is gaining vast popularity through the word of mouth.
With a view to reach out to new horizons, we have set up a manufacturing unit in Udaipur (Rajasthan) under the brand name of “GoPure”. The unit has ultra-modern technology plant equipped to churn out premium range of ice-creams with unique variants like Sitafal, Chiku, Mango, Strawberry, Roasted Almond, Fruit Sorbets and many more fruit/dry fruit flavours. We have also mastered a very unique kulfi – Stuff Mango (shown in below picture) - First a slice is cut off the top of the Mango so that the seed can be dug out and then it’s replaced with the creamy ice cream mixture. The mango is then sealed with wheat flour, and frozen in a blizzard freezer until it’s more solid than a rock.
